Nurturing Nepal's Tech Industry: Insights from Sixit Bhatta

September 7, 2023

//

8 min read

Introduction

The relentless march of technology knows no bounds, and Nepal is no exception to its transformative power. In a recent conversation with Sixit Bhatta, the founder of Tootle, Nepal's pioneering ride-sharing platform and a prominent tech visionary, we delve into the complexities of Nepal's technology sector and its potential for the future. This article serves as a beacon, illuminating the valuable insights and perspectives shared by Sixit Bhatta while underscoring the pivotal role of technology in propelling Nepal's growth.

Chapter 1: The Education System's Role in Tech Industry Growth

Nepal's Educational Quandary

Nepal's education system, much like its global counterparts, faces intense scrutiny for its influence on the trajectory of the tech industry. Sixit Bhatta casts a discerning eye on a pressing issue – the prevalence of compartmentalized knowledge and a superficial grasp of subjects. His clarion call is clear – it's high time to revamp the education system, with an urgent focus on nurturing critical thinking and fostering profound knowledge.

Shattering Illusions

One of the paramount lessons from Bhatta's discourse is the debunking of the fallacy that merely creating apps can spark a tech revolution. He stresses the need for a holistic approach to tech education, advocating for students to delve deeper, beyond the surface, into the fundamental principles underpinning technological progress.

Chapter 2: The Hype and Implications of AI

The AI Phenomenon

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has seized the global imagination, becoming a ubiquitous topic of conversation in Nepal and beyond. Bhatta introduces a thought-provoking sociobiological perspective: "The problem with humanity is that we have paleolithic emotions, medieval institutions, and god-like technology." This statement encapsulates the intricate challenges posed by the rapid ascent of AI.

The Evolution of Human Cognition

The dialogue progresses to explore the evolution of human cognitive skills and the disruptive influence of AI. Bhatta elucidates that as humanity evolved, our cognitive abilities became more intricate, accompanied by a surge in our brain's energy consumption. However, with AI's ascendancy, a profound shift is underway – AI is assuming responsibility for certain cognitive tasks traditionally performed by humans, thereby presenting unique workforce challenges.

From the Attention Economy to the Surveillance Economy

Bhatta delves into the transformation from the attention economy to the surveillance economy. He elucidates how tech giants like Google, initially driven to organize information, pivoted towards capitalizing on user attention through targeted advertising. This transformation in the digital landscape underscores the intricate interplay between technology, economics, and society.

The Quandaries of AI

The dialogue ventures into the quagmires posed by AI to the tech industry. Bhatta underscores that many are embracing AI without a comprehensive understanding of its enduring repercussions. This frenzied rush is reminiscent of the dot-com boom of the late 1990s. The immediate consequence is the replacement of human cognitive skills by machines, raising profound questions about the future of employment.

Chapter 3: AI Opportunities and Challenges in Nepal

Empowering the Value Chain

In Nepal, akin to the global landscape, AI presents a double-edged sword. Bhatta accentuates the significance of employing AI to bolster value chain participants, rather than relegating them to obsolescence. His vision entails AI serving as a tool to amplify human capabilities and cultivate prospects across various sectors such as agriculture and e-commerce.

A Paradigm Shift in Thought

The dialogue underscores the necessity for a paradigm shift in Nepal's approach to AI. Instead of a relentless pursuit of automation, the country should contemplate the integration of AI into workflows, fostering collaboration between AI and human workforces. This approach ensures that value chain participants evolve, rather than diminish, through the incorporation of AI technologies. Given Nepal's limited job market, it is imperative to explore the harmonious integration of these technologies into existing societal structures, augmenting rather than supplanting them.

The Government's Role

Bhatta's insights shed light on the pivotal role played by the government in nurturing an environment conducive to technological innovation. While tech entrepreneurs and businesses steer innovation, government policies and initiatives can forge an enabling ecosystem. Effective collaboration between the public and private sectors emerges as the cornerstone for fostering an environment propitious for tech entrepreneurship.

Chapter 4: Building Digital Public Infrastructure

The Cornerstone of Progress

A cornerstone for the growth of Nepal's tech industry lies in the establishment of a robust digital public infrastructure. Bhatta draws parallels with India's triumphant development of such infrastructure, emphasizing the need for expansive optical fiber networks and ubiquitous communication links.

Mitigating Corruption and Enhancing Efficiency

Bhatta envisions a digital infrastructure that seamlessly amalgamates diverse databases, offering an antidote to corruption while enhancing overall efficiency. Such an infrastructure goes beyond mere connectivity; it nurtures an integrated ecosystem that bestows equitable access to technology's advantages on all Nepali citizens.

Conclusion:

In summation, Sixit Bhatta's insights serve as a comprehensive compass for nurturing Nepal's tech industry. The dialogue underscores the exigency of educational reform, focusing on cultivating profound knowledge and critical thinking to brace the workforce for an AI-driven future.

The intricacies of AI, its challenges, and opportunities are plumbed to their depths. Bhatta conjures a vision of Nepal wherein technology empowers value chain participants and augments efficiencies across sectors. To breathe life into this vision, robust digital public infrastructure and well-informed policies emerge as indomitable prerequisites.

Nepal's tech industry holds immeasurable potential, awaiting activation through innovation, collaboration, and a commitment to bridging the chasm between paleolithic emotions, medieval institutions, and the transformative prowess of technology. As Nepal continues its odyssey through the digital age, these insights beckon a brighter, technologically empowered future.

IWith an enlightened approach to education, judicious AI integration, government support, and the development of digital infrastructure, Nepal's tech industry can thrive, significantly contributing to the nation's growth and prosperity.
